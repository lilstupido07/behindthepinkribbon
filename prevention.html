Prevention

Prevention is one of the most powerful tools we have in the fight against breast cancer. By making simple lifestyle changes and staying informed, individuals can significantly reduce their risk. Below are some key prevention strategies that have been proven to help lower the chances of developing breast cancer.While no prevention method guarantees complete protection, combining several strategies can greatly reduce risk. Early detection and regular screenings, in combination with healthy habits, offer the best defense. Speak with your healthcare provider to develop a plan thatâ€™s right for you.

Breast Cancer Prevention Methods
Prevention Method Effectiveness Details Recommended For
Regular Exercise High At least 150 minutes of moderate activity weekly Everyone
Healthy Diet Moderate High in fruits, vegetables, whole grains; low in red meat Everyone
Limit Alcohol High Limit to 1 drink or less per day Everyone, especially high-risk individuals
Avoid Smoking High Reduces overall cancer risk Everyone
Maintain Healthy Weight High Especially important after menopause Women 40+
Breastfeeding Moderate May slightly lower the risk, especially long-term New mothers
Regular Screenings Very High (for early detection) Mammograms and clinical breast exams Women 40+ or high risk
